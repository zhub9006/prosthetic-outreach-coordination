## Meeting Point Analysis

The geographic center of our three cities is approximately **39.33°N, 81.02°W**, which falls in **rural Ohio** near Hebron. The nearest major city is **Columbus, Ohio** (~39.96°N, 83.00°W).

### Why Columbus, OH?

| Factor | Detail |
|--------|--------|
| **Geographic fairness** | Columbus is the closest major city to the geographic midpoint of all three teams |
| **Drive times** | Chicago → Columbus ~6 hrs; Boston → Columbus ~8.5 hrs; Atlanta → Columbus ~9.5 hrs |
| **Air travel** | ~1.5–2 hrs flight from each city to Columbus (CMH airport) |
| **Venue options** | Downtown Columbus offers co-working spaces, restaurants, hotels, and community venues |
| **Cost** | Mid-range for meeting venues; accessible by air and car |

### Recommended Meeting Venues in Columbus, OH

#### Co-working Spaces
- **WeWork** — 640 Mound St, Downtown Columbus
- **Impact Hub Columbus** — 120 Cleveland Ave, AI-powered community workspace
- **Industrious** — 515 S Front St, upscale flexible workspace

#### Restaurants (Downtown)
- **The Capital Grille** — upscale, private dining rooms available
- **dodos coffee** — casual, open workspace-friendly
- **King's Dorum** — Mediterranean, good for group dining

#### Hotels (Downtown)
- **Hilton Columbus Downtown** — 50 W Spring St, walking distance to venues
- **Hyatt Regency Columbus** — 151 E Gay St, large meeting rooms available

#### Community & Cultural Venues
- **COSI (Center of Science and Industry)** — 3333 W Broad St, unique venue, wheelchair accessible
- **Columbus Metropolitan Library** — 96 S Grant Ave, free meeting rooms available
- **Ohio Theatre** — 55 S Pearl St, historic venue

## Meeting Cadence
- **Weekly**: Virtual standup (30 min)
- **Monthly**: All-hands sync (1 hr, rotates between cities or Columbus)
- **Quarterly**: In-person strategy meeting in Columbus

## Travel Tips
- Book flights early for best CMH rates from ORD, BOS, and ATL
- Columbus has good highway access (I-70, I-71, I-270) for driving teams
- Consider lodging packages through Hilton or Hyatt for quarterly in-person meetings
- Virtual-first recommended for weekly/monthly syncs; reserve Columbus for quarterly strategic planning
