## Meeting Point
The geographic center of our three cities is approximately **39.33°N, 81.02°W**, which falls in **rural Ohio** near Hebron. The nearest major city is **Columbus, Ohio** (~39.96°N, 83.00°W).

### Recommended Meeting Areas
- **Columbus, OH** — Best compromise for in-person meetups
  - Downtown Columbus has numerous meeting venues (restaurants, co-working spaces, hotels)
  - ~6-hour drive from Chicago, ~8 hours from Boston, ~10 hours from Atlanta
  - Alternative: Virtual meetings with quarterly in-person gatherings in Columbus

## Suggested Meeting Venues in Columbus, OH
- **Co-working spaces**: WeWork, Industrious, or Impact Hub Columbus
- **Restaurants**: The Capital Grille, dodos, or King's durum (downtown)
- **Hotels**: Hilton Columbus Downtown, Hyatt Regency Columbus
- **Community centers**: COSI (Center of Science and Industry), Columbus Metropolitan Library

## Meeting Cadence
- **Weekly**: Virtual standup (30 min)
- **Monthly**: All-hands sync (1 hr, rotates between cities or Columbus)
- **Quarterly**: In-person strategy meeting in Columbus
