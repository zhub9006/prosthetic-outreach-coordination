# Prosthetic Outreach Coordination

Central hub for coordinating volunteer tasks, outreach schedules, and team collaboration for the prosthetic outreach initiative.

## Mission
To coordinate volunteer efforts across teams in **Chicago, Boston, and Atlanta** to support affordable prosthetic solutions and improve access to prosthetic care. Our initiative focuses on connecting clinical research, community outreach, and volunteer coordination to make prosthetic technology more accessible and affordable.

## 💡 Key Findings from Clinical Trials

- **3D printing + AI** can dramatically reduce prosthetic manufacturing costs while maintaining or improving performance
- **Reverse innovation** (bring low-cost solutions from developing countries to high-income settings) is a promising cost-reduction strategy
- **Affordable socket fabrication** using expandable foam can cut fitting time from hours to minutes
- **Additive manufacturing** of prosthetic feet can match commercial carbon-fiber performance at lower cost
