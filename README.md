# Prosthetic Outreach Coordination

Central hub for coordinating volunteer tasks, outreach schedules, and team collaboration for the prosthetic outreach initiative.

## Mission
To coordinate volunteer efforts across teams in Chicago, Boston, and Atlanta to support affordable prosthetic solutions and improve access to prosthetic care.

## Getting Started
- Review the [Volunteer Task Board](ISSUES.md) for current tasks
- Check the [Outreach Calendar](OUTREACH.md) for scheduled events
- See [Resources](RESOURCES.md) for clinical trial data and research links

## Team Locations
- **Chicago Team**: Shirley Ryan AbilityLab area (41.8756°N, 87.6244°W)
- **Boston Team**: Collaborating institutions (42.3588°N, 71.0578°W)
- **Atlanta Team**: Outreach coordination hub (33.7545°N, 84.3898°W)

## Meeting Point
Our virtual and in-person meetings rotate. The geographic center of our three teams falls in **rural Ohio** near Hebron, with the nearest major city being **Columbus, Ohio** — approximately equidistant from all three locations.
