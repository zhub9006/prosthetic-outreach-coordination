# Prosthetic Outreach Initiative — Coordination Hub

**Central hub to coordinate volunteer tasks for the prosthetic outreach initiative across Chicago, Boston, and Atlanta teams — task tracking, team coordination, and resource management for affordable prosthetic solutions.**

## 📍 Teams
- **Chicago** — [Tasks & Progress](https://github.com/orgs/prosthetic-outreach-initiative/projects)
- **Boston** — [Tasks & Progress](https://github.com/orgs/prosthetic-outreach-initiative/projects)
- **Atlanta** — [Tasks & Progress](https://github.com/orgs/prosthetic-outreach-initiative/projects)

## 🎯 Focus Areas
1. **Low-Cost Prosthetic Devices** — Managing inventory and distribution of affordable prosthetic solutions
2. **Clinical Trials Coordination** — Identifying and enrolling participants in prosthetic-related trials
3. **Community Outreach** — Connecting with underserved communities
4. **Fundraising & Grants** — Securing funding for affordable prosthetic solutions
5. **Technical Support** — Device fitting, maintenance, and training
