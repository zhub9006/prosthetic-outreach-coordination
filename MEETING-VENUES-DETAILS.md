# Detailed Venue Information

## Greater Columbus Convention Center

- **Address**: 400 North High Street, Columbus, OH 43215
- **Coordinates**: 39.9719°N, -83.0011°W
- **Capacity**: Up to 2,000+ for plenary sessions
- **Rooms**: Multiple breakout rooms, exhibit hall
- **Amenities**: High-speed wifi, AV equipment, catering
- **Parking**: Attached garages, street parking
- **Public Transit**: COTA bus lines nearby
- **Best For**: Large coordination meetings, presentations, workshops
