## Recommended Meeting Strategies

### Option 1: Columbus, OH — In-Person Hub
Columbus is the best compromise for quarterly in-person meetings. It's centrally located and well-connected by air, rail, and highway.

**Downtown Columbus Meeting Venues:**
- **Co-working spaces**: WeWork, Impact Hub Columbus
- **Hotel conference rooms**: Hilton Columbus Downtown, Hyatt Regency Columbus
- **Community venues**: COSI (Center of Science and Industry), Columbus Metropolitan Library
- **Restaurant private dining**: The Capital Grille, dodos, King's durum (all in the Short North/downtown area)

### Option 2: Rotating City Meetings
Meet in each city on a rotating basis:
- Q1: Chicago (Shirley Ryan AbilityLab area)
- Q2: Boston
- Q3: Atlanta
- Q4: Virtual or Columbus

### Option 3: Virtual-First with Annual In-Person
- Weekly virtual standups (30 min)
- Monthly all-hands sync (1 hr)
- Annual in-person retreat in Columbus

## Drive Times from Columbus
- Columbus → Chicago: ~6 hours
- Columbus → Boston: ~8.5 hours
- Columbus → Atlanta: ~9.5 hours
- Columbus → each city by air: ~1.5–2 hours

## Cost Considerations
For budget-conscious outreach, virtual meetings reduce travel costs significantly. If in-person meetings are necessary, Columbus offers the lowest average travel cost across all three teams.
