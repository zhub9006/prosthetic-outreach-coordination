# Meeting Point & Venue Guide

## Geographic Midpoint Analysis

The geographic center of our three teams (Chicago, Boston, Atlanta) falls near **Columbus, Ohio**, making it the optimal in-person meeting hub.

### Drive Times from Each City

| City | Distance | Drive Time | Flight Time |
|------|----------|------------|-------------|
| **Chicago** | ~510 km (~317 mi) | ~6.8 hours | ~1.5 hours |
| **Boston** | ~1,227 km (~762 mi) | ~14.6 hours | ~2 hours |
| **Atlanta** | ~896 km (~557 mi) | ~10.7 hours | ~1.5 hours |

### Recommended Meeting Venues in Columbus, OH

#### 🏢 Conference & Convention Venues

1. **Greater Columbus Convention Center**
   - 📍 400 North High Street, Arena District, Downtown Columbus
   - 🎯 Best for large group meetings, presentations, and workshops
   - 🏷️ Conference centre with multiple breakout rooms
   - ⭐ Central downtown location, walkable to hotels and restaurants

2. **Hilton Columbus Downtown**
   - 📍 402 North High Street, Arena District, Downtown Columbus
   - 🎯 Ideal for overnight accommodations + meeting space
   - 🏷️ Hotel with extensive conference facilities
   - ⭐ Connected to the Convention Center; 4-star amenities

3. **Sheraton Columbus Hotel at Capitol Square**
   - 📍 755 East State Street, Capitol Square
   - 🎯 State-of-the-art meeting and event spaces
   - 🏷️ Near Ohio State University; good for academic collaboration

4. **Hyatt Regency Columbus**
   - 📍 361 South High Street, German Village
   - 🎯 Modern conference facilities in historic German Village
   - 🏷️ Largest ballroom in downtown Columbus; 2,200 capacity

#### 🍽️ Dining & Informal Meeting Venues

1. **High Street Diner** (Arena District)
   - Casual, group-friendly breakfast/lunch spot near Convention Center

2. **Lindey's on High** (Short North)
   - American bistro, suitable for team dinners

3. **The Strip Club** (Short North)
   - Trendy gastropub with group seating options

4. **Max & Erma's** (Arena District)
   - American comfort food, group-friendly, near Convention Center

## Meeting Schedule Recommendations

### Virtual-First Approach
- Weekly syncs via video call (Zoom/Teams)
- Monthly deeper working sessions

### Quarterly In-Person Meetings
- **Frequency**: Every 3 months
- **Duration**: Full day (9 AM - 5 PM)
- **Format**: Morning planning + afternoon site visits
- **Venue rotation**: Primary = Convention Center; backup = Ohio State facilities

### Suggested Quarterly Locations

| Quarter | Focus | Recommended Venue |
|---------|-------|-------------------|
| Q1 | Planning & Goal Setting | Convention Center |
| Q2 | Mid-year Review & Training | Ohio State / Wexner Medical Center area |
| Q3 | Outreach Coordination | Convention Center |
| Q4 | Annual Review & Planning | Sheraton Capitol Square |
