## Recommended Meeting Venues in Columbus, OH

### Co-Working Spaces
- **WeWork** – Multiple downtown locations, flexible hourly/day passes
- **Industrious** – Executive suites with meeting room rentals
- **Impact Hub Columbus** – Social impact-focused space, ideal for outreach initiatives

### Hotels (Downtown)
- **Hilton Columbus Downtown** – Central location, meeting rooms available
- **Hyatt Regency Columbus** – Riverfront location, large event capacity
- **Drury Inn & Suites Columbus** – Budget-friendly option

### Restaurants & Cafes (Downtown)
- **The Capital Grille** – Upscale, private dining rooms
- **King's Durum** – Mediterranean, casual meeting-friendly
- **Dodos** – Local favorite, good for group lunches
- **Katalina** – Latin fusion, group-friendly

### Community & Educational Venues
- **COSI (Center of Science and Industry)** – Iconic Columbus venue, inspirational setting
- **Columbus Metropolitan Library Main Branch** – Free meeting rooms, central location
- **Columbus State Community College** – Affordable rental spaces

## Meeting Cadence
- **Weekly**: Virtual standup (30 min)
- **Monthly**: All-hands sync (1 hr, rotates between cities or Columbus)
- **Quarterly**: In-person strategy meeting in Columbus, OH
