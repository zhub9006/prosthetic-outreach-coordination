# Contributing to the Prosthetic Outreach Initiative

## How to Get Involved

We welcome volunteers from all backgrounds — clinicians, engineers, students, community organizers, and advocates.

### Volunteer Task Categories
1. **Prosthetic Device Distribution** — Managing inventory and delivery of affordable prosthetic devices
2. **Clinical Trials Coordination** — Identifying and enrolling participants in prosthetic-related trials
3. **Outreach & Community Engagement** — Connecting with underserved communities in Chicago, Boston, and Atlanta
4. **Fundraising & Grants** — Securing funding for affordable prosthetic solutions
5. **Technical Support** — Device fitting, maintenance, and user training

### Getting Started
1. Review our [open issues](https://github.com/zhub9006/prosthetic-outreach-coordination/issues)
2. Claim a task by commenting on the issue
3. Join our weekly coordination call (schedule TBD)
4. Report progress in your assigned issue
