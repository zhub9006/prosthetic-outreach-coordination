## Clinical Trials for Affordable Prosthetic Solutions

Based on research from [Prosthetic Access Atlas](https://github.com/zhub9006/prosthetic-access-atlas) — an analysis of **2,162** prosthetic-related clinical trials from ClinicalTrials.gov.

### Key Statistics from Clinical Trials Database
| Metric | Value |
|--------|-------|
| **Total registered trials (prosthetic)** | 2,162 |
| **Recruiting trials** | ~378 (17.4%) |
| **Completed trials** | ~923 (42.7%) |
| **Trials with sites in underserved regions** | 0 |

### Innovative Prosthetic Solutions Identified
- **3D-Printed Prosthetics** — LIMBER UniLeg and other open-source designs
- **Neural-Controlled Prostheses** — Osseointegrated (e-OPRA), EMG-controlled systems
- **Adaptive Prosthetic Foot** — Variable stiffness (VSPA Foot)
- **Telerehabilitation** — Remote prosthetic rehab programs
- **Wireless EMG Control** — ASTERISK System

### Key Gap: Underserved Regions
**Zero prosthetic or orthotic care providers** were identified within 50 km of:
- Rural West Virginia (~100 km to nearest prosthetist)
- Eastern Kentucky (~130 km to nearest prosthetist)  
- Mississippi Delta (~180 km to nearest prosthetist)
